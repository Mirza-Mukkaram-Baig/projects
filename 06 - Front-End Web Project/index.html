Missions

“It doesn’t matter what you do, if you live and die as you like. However, no matter what road you end up taking, remember to protect the people that are precious to you.” – Sarutobi Hiruzen

Chunin Exams

The exams are a chance for ninja to be promoted from genin to chunin. The tests also serve as a chance for the various ninja villages to spy on each other. Kakashi has his team sign up to compete. Sasuke, Sakura, and Naruto are put through a series of exams that push them to their limits. All three have a near-death experience at some point during the exam. The exams also prove to be a changing point for Sasuke. As a result of his experiences, he decides to leave the village forever.

Golden Statue

Another flashback from Shippuden shows another mission with Team 7. Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura are sent to recover a golden statue that had been stolen. Sasuke and Naruto end up with their dominant hands trapped and stuck together. The two have to find a way to work together and save a captured Sakura. The two genin have to show incredible teamwork skills to save their friend. In the end, Sasuke and Naruto find out they will be stuck together for a few days.

Mission With Gaara

When Gaara is introduced, he is full of anger and rage. His encounter with Naruto sends him on a different path. In an episode of Shippuden, a flashback shows Gaara volunteering to team up with a group from Konoha. Team 7, along with Gaara and a couple of Sand Ninja, are dispatched to deal with a border dispute. Gaara has to work to earn the trust of those around him. His teammates distrust him because of his status as a jinchuriki while Team 7 is shocked to see how much Gaara has changed since they last saw him during the events of the Chunin Exams.

The Reunion

Team 7 is brought together for the first time since Sasuke left. Naruto and Sakura are given a chance to see their lost teammate for the first time in years. Team 7 has been reborn with Yamato filling in for Kakashi and Sai taking over for Sasuke. Sasuke proves that he has become stronger than both Naruto and Sakura. Sai seems to be the only one who stands a chance at defeating the rogue ninja. Naruto and Sakura fail in their mission to get Sasuke to return to Konoha.

Naruto's Comrades

Shikamaru Nara

Big Brain

Shikamaru Nara (奈良シカマル, Nara Shikamaru) is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Nara clan. Though lazy by nature, Shikamaru has a rare intellect that consistently allows him to prevail in combat. The responsibilities that these successes leave him with cause him frequent annoyance, but he gladly accepts them so that he may be of service to his fellow members of Team Asuma, and to prove himself to generations of the past and future.

Sakura Haruno

Billboard Head

Sakura Uchiha (うちはサクラ, Uchiha Sakura, née Haruno (春野)) is a kunoichi of Konohagakure. When assigned to Team 7, Sakura quickly finds herself ill-prepared for the duties of a shinobi. However, after training under the Sannin Tsunade, she overcomes this, and becomes recognised as one of the greatest medical-nin in the world.

Minato Namikaze

Yellow Flash of the Leaf

Minato Namikaze (波風ミナト, Namikaze Minato) was the Fourth Hokage (四代目火影, Yondaime Hokage, literally meaning: Fourth Fire Shadow) of Konohagakure. He was renowned all over the world as Konoha's Yellow Flash (木ノ葉の黄色い閃光, Konoha no Kiiroi Senkō, English TV: Yellow Flash of the Leaf). He died during the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox's Attack, sacrificing his life to seal a part of the Nine-Tails into his newborn son, Naruto Uzumaki.

Kushina Uzumaki

Red Hair

Kushina Uzumaki (うずまきクシナ, Uzumaki Kushina) was a Konohagakure kunoichi who originated from Uzushiogakure's Uzumaki clan. She was the second jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails. And mother of Naruto Uzamaki

Jiraiya

Pervi Sage

Jiraiya (自来也) was one of Konohagakure's Sannin. Famed as a hermit and pervert of stupendous ninja skill, Jiraiya travelled the world in search of knowledge that would help his friends, the various novels he wrote, and, posthumously, the world in its entirety – knowledge that would be passed on to his godson and final student, Naruto Uzumaki.

Saskue Uchiha

The Last Surviving Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha (うちはサスケ, Uchiha Sasuke) is one of the last surviving members of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. After his older brother, Itachi, slaughtered their clan, Sasuke made it his mission in life to avenge them by killing Itachi. He is added to Team 7 upon becoming a ninja and, through competition with his rival and best friend, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke starts developing his skills.
